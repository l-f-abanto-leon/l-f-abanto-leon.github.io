About Me

I am PhD student at Technische Universität Darmstadt. My research focuses on millimeter-wave for to Industry 4.0. More specfically, I am working on the intergration of NOMA, massive MIMO, hybrid precoding and scheduling for massive IoT communications.

Research Interests

  • millimeter-wave communications, hybrid precoding, signal processing,
  • optimization, non-orthogonal multiple access (NOMA), industrial IoT,
  • cellular vehicle-to-everything (C-V2X), wireless link scheduling, artificial intelligence
